How To Use

1. Show

move cursor on 'Show' button and categories will appear.

select category you want.

then it will show you the list of the bookmark in the category.

you can click bookmark to go to the link.


2. Categorize

click 'Categorize' button and uncategorized bookmarks will appear.

check bookmarks you want to categorize and click 'submit' button below.

it will categorize selected bookmarks.


If you want to see this tutorial again, click 'Home' button.
